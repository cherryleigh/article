⚲ Marikina City

Our Community in the next Generations

Many communities in the Philippines face issues that take years to resolve. Problems such as flooding, traffic, and waste management continue to affect residents every day. In my opinion, these issues take too long to solve because solutions are often delayed or not consistently followed.

One reason these problems remain is the lack of long-term planning and cooperation. For example, flooding still happens in some areas during heavy rains because drainage systems are not always maintained properly. Another reason is that some residents ignore rules like proper waste disposal, which can worsen environmental problems.

To address these challenges, local leaders should focus on stronger planning and faster action when problems appear. If everyone works together and follows community rules, long-standing problems can finally be solved.
