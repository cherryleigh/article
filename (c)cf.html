⚲ Marikina City

Top 5 Community Issues

  1. Traffic Congestion – Many cities experience heavy traffic every day, especially in places like Manila and Quezon City. This causes long travel times for workers and students.
  2. Flooding During Heavy Rain – Flooding often happens during the rainy season because of clogged drainage systems and improper waste disposal in many communities.
  3. Waste management – Improper disposal of garbage contributes to clogged drainage systems and environmental problems.
  4. Limited Job Opportunities – Many people find it difficult to get stable jobs, especially in rural areas. This can lead to poverty and migration to bigger cities.
  5. Access to Quality Education – Some schools lack enough classrooms, learning materials, or technology, which affects the quality of education students receive.

Did You Know?

Local Statistic: Marikina City has one of the largest river park systems in Metro Manila, with Marikina River Park stretching several kilometers where residents can exercise, bike, and enjoy outdoor activities.

Historical Fact: The first pair of locally made leather shoes in Marikina was created by Don Laureano “Kapitan Moy” Guevarra, who helped start the city’s shoe industry.

Fun Fact: Marikina is known for its clean streets and organized neighborhoods, and the city government strictly encourages proper waste segregation among residents and students.
